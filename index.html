OUR SYSTEMATIC APPROACH

Our Hiring Process

PHASE 01 Days 1 - 3

Talent Sourcing

We identify and engage high-potential passive candidates through our global tech network and premium sourcing channels.

Key Activities:
  • Multi-channel search (LinkedIn, GitHub, Stack Overflow)
  • Deep-tech community talent mapping
Milestone: Qualified Candidate Pool Assembled
1
2
PHASE 02 Days 4 - 6

Screening & Shortlisting

A rigorous filter stage checking core tech stack competencies, soft skill dynamics, culture fit, and career goals.

Key Activities:
  • In-depth recruiter pre-screening interview
  • Branding and motivation alignment check
Milestone: Approved Longlist of Verified Talents
PHASE 03 Days 7 - 10

Interview & Assessment

Candidates undergo structured technical evaluations, code challenges, and expert assessments conducted by veteran tech leads.

Key Activities:
  • Specialist coding peer audits and case studies
  • Consolidated scoring reports & video profiles
Milestone: Peer-Vetted Shortlist & Assessment Dossiers
3
4
PHASE 04 Days 11 - 13

Selection & Offer

Final selection round with stakeholders, followed by reference checks, salary benchmarking guidance, and formal contract offers.

Key Activities:
  • Consolidated review and interview debriefs
  • Salary negotiations, compliance check, contract draft
Milestone: Offer Letter Extended & Accepted
PHASE 05 Day 14+

Onboarding & Support

Seamless onboarding transition supported by our HR teams, followed by scheduled check-ins to guarantee long-term retention.

Key Activities:
  • First day IT/compliance check-ins
  • 30, 60, and 90-day progress checkups
Milestone: Candidate Fully Integrated & Performing
